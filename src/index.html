Welcome

For almost 25 years, our small clinic has been home to a select group of extraordinary massage therapists. We are specialists in the area of massage known as injury treatment. Our modalities includes craniosacral, neurofascial and intraoral massage, as well as neuromuscular therapy, pregnancy, deep tissue, and sports massage. All done with big hearts, strong hands, and sharp minds.

The name of the modality is never as important as the nature of the practitioner. Our richness and rarity is our collection of therapists, practicing in a coordinated clinic. We are strongly grounded in massage research and science. The deeply healthful work we offer may be passive for one patient and quite active for the next, often including instruction in therapeutic exercise. Our goal is always to help you reach your highest ones.

From your first phone call, our group works collaboratively to match you with members of our team. In this regard, too, we are uncompromising and unparalleled. In most instances, we can only accept new patients who are referred directly to us.
